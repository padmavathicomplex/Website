### Step 1: Create Google Business Profile (CRITICAL!)
This is the **most important** step to appear in Google searches and Google Maps.

1. **Go to**: https://business.google.com
2. **Sign in** with your Gmail account (padmavathicomplex@gmail.com)
3. **Click "Manage now"** or "Add your business"
4. **Enter business details**:
   - Business name: **Padmavathi Complex**
   - Category: **Lodging** or **Guest House**
   - Location: **Thirumanjana Street, Thirupugalur, Nagapattinam District, Tamil Nadu 609704**
   - Phone: **+91 63828 12143**
   - Website: **https://padmavathicomplex.com**
5. **Verify your business** (Google will send a postcard with verification code to your address)
6. **Add photos** of your rooms, property, and facilities
7. **Add business hours**: 24/7
8. **Add description** focusing on "rooms in Thirupugalur near Agnipureeswarar Temple"

### Step 2: Submit to Google Search Console
1. **Go to**: https://search.google.com/search-console
2. **Add your website**: padmavathicomplex.com
3. **Verify ownership** (use HTML tag method or DNS verification)
4. **Submit sitemap**: Submit `https://padmavathicomplex.com/sitemap.xml`
5. **Request indexing** for your homepage

### Step 3: Get Listed in Online Directories
Submit your business to:
- **JustDial**: https://www.justdial.com (Search "Add Business")
- **Sulekha**: https://www.sulekha.com
- **IndiaMART**: https://www.indiamart.com
- **TripAdvisor**: https://www.tripadvisor.in (for accommodation)
- **MakeMyTrip**: https://www.makemytrip.com (partner program)

### Step 4: Build Local Citations

- Google Business Profile
